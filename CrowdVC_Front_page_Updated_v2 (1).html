Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first investment

What's the minimum investment amount?
Minimum investment varies by pool, typically $5K-$10K. This allows meaningful diversification while keeping entry barriers low for retail investors.
How does the AI scoring work?
Our proprietary algorithm analyzes 127+ data points including team experience, market size, traction metrics, unit economics, and competitive positioning. Each startup receives a 0-100 score with transparent breakdown.
Is this Shariah-compliant?
Yes. Select pools are certified halal by our Islamic finance advisory board. We screen for riba (interest), gharar (uncertainty), and haram business models. Investments are equity-based with profit-sharing structures.
How secure are my funds?
Funds are escrowed in audited smart contracts on Arbitrum. We use multi-sig wallets, institutional custody options, and have passed security audits from leading blockchain firms. Your capital never touches centralized accounts.
When do I receive returns?
Returns are distributed upon liquidity events (acquisition, IPO, or secondary sale). Average holding period is 3-5 years. You'll receive equity tokens representing your ownership stake immediately after pool closes.
Can I sell my equity tokens early?
Secondary marketplace coming Q2 2025. You'll be able to trade equity tokens peer-to-peer with price discovery based on startup performance and market demand.
